COZY CRAFT

"Cozy Craft" is upcoming wholesome game about discovering colorful worlds by collecting resources.

Family friendly and non violent gameplay with super easy understandable controls and additional couch co-op gameplay up to 4 players.

Worlds are procedurally generated so each game is unique, you can play forever and never get bored!

Game engine: Unity
