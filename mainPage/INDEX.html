MERGE AND COLLAPSE

World has been attacked! Invaders started in the USA but they certainly won't stop there, so they must be resisted in America. These mysterious red humanoids are building their strongholds everywhere and it will be up to you to destroy them. This is crucial to our victory!

Will you do it?

Buy Cubes and merge them to increase their capabilities. We will reward you with special abilities for effective merging. Their endless combinations will make each fight unique. Also, our intel provides numerous multipliers, so remember to use them effectively. Every Cube counts on the battlefield!

Enemies are indestructible, but we can destroy their strongholds. Search for and attack weak points in their structures. We will win!

Bullet Fusion is a unique combination of a puzzle game and a roguelike. Merging, multipliers and physics based destructible structures! This mix makes game a lot of fun.
